Our Story

Schrodinger Technologie Ltd. is a Nigerian-based social enterprise committed to solving one of humanity’s most urgent challenges ensuring universal access to clean water while fostering environmental sustainability and climate resilience. Established in 2023 with a clear mission to transform water access in underserved and climate-vulnerable communities, we work at the intersection of renewable energy, water technology, and social impact to deliver innovative, sustainable, and affordable solutions.

Our organization is driven by the belief that access to clean water is a fundamental human right. We aim to break the cycle of water poverty by developing and implementing systems that are not only effective but also adaptable to the unique social, cultural, and environmental contexts of the communities we serve.

We operate with a people-first approach, engaging communities from the design phase through implementation to ensure local ownership, sustainability, and maximum impact. Our model combines technical innovation with social enterprise principles, ensuring affordability while creating local employment and capacity-building opportunities.

Schrodinger Technologie is deeply aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals particularly SDG 6 (Clean Water & Sanitation), SDG 3 (Good Health & Well-being), SDG 7 (Affordable & Clean Energy), and SDG 13 (Climate Action). We address water scarcity not as an isolated issue but as a gateway to broader development outcomes, including improved public health, reduced inequality, and climate adaptation.

Since inception, we have made measurable strides improving access to safe drinking water for thousands of people, creating jobs, reducing reliance on fossil fuels, and advocating for climate-conscious policies. Our impact is amplified through strategic partnerships with governments, development agencies, non-profits, and private sector actors who share our vision for a water-secure, sustainable, and equitable future.

Recognized nationally and internationally for our innovative approach, Schrodinger Technologie continues to push boundaries in the water and sustainability sectors. We are not just a company; we are a movement driven by purpose, powered by innovation, and committed to building a future where no one is left behind in the quest for clean, safe water.

Our Mission

To enhance lives globally by providing access to clean and safe water, empowering communities to lead healthier, more productive lives.

Our Vision

To create a world where everyone has access to Clean Water and Energy, regardless of their Location or Economic Circumstances.
