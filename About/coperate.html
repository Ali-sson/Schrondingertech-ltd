Corporate Governance Framework for Schrodinger Technologies

Basic Policy – At Schrodinger Technologies, our corporate philosophy is built on the pillars of innovation, transparency, and integrity. We are committed to fostering sustainable development through cutting-edge technology in renewable energy, water purification, and environmental solutions. Our mission is to create a lasting impact by empowering communities, fostering economic growth, and advancing technological excellence.

To achieve our mission, a strong corporate governance structure is essential. Our governance framework is designed to ensure effective decision-making, enhance accountability, and uphold the highest ethical standards in all our operations. This foundation enables us to drive long-term growth, deliver value to stakeholders, and maintain trust within the industry and the communities we serve.

Corporate Governance Principles

1. Ensuring Shareholder Rights and Fairness

Schrodinger Technologies is committed to safeguarding shareholder rights by adhering to all regulatory frameworks and ensuring equal treatment of all shareholders, including minority and foreign investors. We actively promote transparency, accessibility, and fairness, enabling all shareholders to exercise their rights and participate in decision-making processes.

2. Stakeholder Engagement and Corporate Social Responsibility

Recognizing that sustainable growth requires a holistic approach, we prioritize strong relationships with all stakeholders—employees, customers, investors, partners, and the communities we serve. Our business philosophy of "sustainable innovation for societal impact" drives our CSR commitment.

We have established a Business Conduct Policy and Code of Ethics to guide ethical practices. Our projects align with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), ensuring our innovations positively impact society and the environment.

3. Transparency and Disclosure of Information

We are dedicated to honest and transparent decision-making, ensuring stakeholders have access to accurate financial and operational information. We disclose key aspects such as:

  • Financial performance and business outcomes
  • Corporate strategy, management plans, and initiatives
  • Governance structure, risk management, and compliance policies

4. Board of Directors: Responsibilities and Structure

The Board of Directors plays a fundamental role in shaping the long-term vision and strategy. Key responsibilities include defining policies, ensuring compliance, promoting ethical culture, and overseeing risk management.

  • Defining and overseeing the company’s strategic plans and policies
  • Ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory frameworks
  • Promoting ethical corporate culture and accountability
  • Implementing internal control and risk management frameworks

To strengthen governance, we have appointed independent directors with expertise in corporate management, technology, and sustainable development. Their diverse backgrounds ensure comprehensive oversight and decision-making. Additionally, we have established a Nomination and Remuneration Committee, composed of independent members, to oversee leadership succession planning, director appointments, and executive compensation.

5. Ethical Business Conduct and Compliance

Integrity is a core value at Schrodinger Technologies. We have developed a robust compliance and ethics program, including:

  • Corporate Code of Conduct
  • Whistleblower policy for safe reporting
  • Regular compliance training

Our internal audit function monitors adherence to ethical and legal standards, ensuring continuous compliance with national and international regulations.

6. Dialogue with Investors and Stakeholders

To foster trust and alignment with our mission, we maintain open and constructive dialogue with investors, customers, and partners. Our communication strategy includes:

  • Regular investor meetings and briefings
  • Annual general meetings with transparent reporting
  • Transparent engagement with regulatory bodies and industry partners
  • Community outreach programs to integrate stakeholder feedback

7. Corporate Governance System and Decision-Making Structure

Our governance model ensures efficient decision-making and operational execution. The Board of Directors convenes monthly, providing strategic oversight and assessing business performance. The Executive Leadership Team is responsible for day-to-day management and execution of corporate strategy, ensuring agility in business operations..

Additionally, we hold bi-weekly management meetings where key executives, department heads, and the Audit & Risk Committee assess progress, mitigate risks, and align on strategic initiatives.

8. Risk Management and Sustainability

Risk management is integral to our corporate governance framework. We have implemented a structured approach to identifying, assessing, and mitigating risks across all aspects of our operations, including:

  • Financial and operational risk assessment
  • Cybersecurity and data protection strategies
  • Environmental sustainability and compliance with environmental regulations

Our sustainability initiatives focus on energy efficiency, reducing carbon footprint, and developing eco-friendly technologies that align with our mission of promoting clean Water and renewable energy solutions.

At Schrodinger Technologies, we believe that strong corporate governance is the foundation of sustainable growth, innovation, and societal impact. Through ethical leadership, transparency, and a commitment to excellence, we strive to drive positive change while delivering long-term value to our shareholders, employees, customers, and communities. Our governance principles will continue to evolve to meet emerging challenges and opportunities, ensuring we remain at the forefront of transformative solutions for a sustainable future.
