Our Services

At Schrodinger Technologies, we provide a complete suite of services designed to solve water and energy challenges across communities,
industries, and businesses.

Hydro-Geological Survey

Every successful water project begins with understanding the earth beneath us. Our hydro-geological survey service provides precise analysis of underground water resources, soil structure, and aquifer potential. Using advanced tools and expertise, we help clients determine the best locations for boreholes, wells, and water stations. This reduces risks, lowers costs, and ensures sustainable water sourcing. Whether for rural development, industries, or private estates, our surveys provide the scientific foundation for reliable water access.

Borehole Drilling

Access to clean groundwater is essential for homes, businesses, and communities. Our borehole drilling service combines technical expertise, modern equipment, and sustainable practices to deliver highquality water solutions. From site assessment to drilling, casing, and pump installation, we provide end-to-end services tailored to client needs. Our goal is to ensure safe, long-lasting, and affordable water supply systems that meet daily demands while protecting the environment. With us, boreholes are not just drilled—they’re built to serve generations.

Community Water Stations

Water is life, and access should not be a privilege. Our community water stations are designed to provide safe, affordable, and easily accessible drinking water for towns, villages, and peri-urban communities. Powered by solar and equipped with advanced purification systems, these stations reduce reliance on unsafe water sources, prevent waterborne diseases, and empower communities with sustainable access. Each project is tailored to community needs, creating impact where it matters most—at the grassroots.

Building and Industrial Plumbing

Reliable plumbing is the backbone of any modern building or industrial facility. We design and install plumbing systems that guarantee efficiency, durability, and compliance with safety standards. From residential estates to hospitals, hotels, and factories, our services cover water distribution, sewage systems, and industrial piping. We don’t just install pipes—we create smart, sustainable networks that keep water flowing where it’s needed most, with minimal maintenance and maximum. reliability.

Wastewater Treatment

Water scarcity demands smarter solutions. Our wastewater treatment services transform polluted or used water into clean, reusable water for industries, agriculture, and communities. By combining biological, chemical, and mechanical processes, we eliminate harmful contaminants while recovering water for sustainable use. Whether for industrial discharge, municipal wastewater, or commercial facilities, our systems are tailored for efficiency and compliance. This service not only reduces environmental impact but also helps clients save costs and embrace sustainability.

Solar Installation

Energy and water go hand in hand. Our solar installation services provide renewable power solutions for homes, industries, and water systems. From standalone solar panels to hybrid energy setups, we design and install systems that cut energy costs, reduce carbon footprints, and ensure reliable power supply. Solar-driven water pumps and purification units are among our specialties, combining clean energy with safe water delivery. With us, clients enjoy the freedom of energy independence and sustainability.

Irrigation Farming Setup

Agriculture thrives on water management. Our irrigation setup services deliver efficient, modern solutions that help farmers optimize water use, boost yields, and reduce waste. From drip irrigation systems to solar-powered pumping and distribution networks, we ensure farms get the right water supply at the right time. Our setups support food security, climate resilience, and profitability for both smallholder and commercial farmers. With Schrodinger Technologies, farming becomes smarter, greener, and more. productive.
