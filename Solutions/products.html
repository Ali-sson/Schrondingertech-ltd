Our Products

All our systems are energy-efficient, solar-powered, and climate-resilient, built with advanced technology, multi-stage filtration,
and UV sterilization. Many are IoT-enabled for real-time monitoring of water quality, including TDS, pH, turbidity, and flow rate.

UVC Disinfection Bottle

Safe water, anytime and anywhere. Our UVC Disinfection Bottle is designed for individuals on the go—travelers, students, and professionals. With a built-in UVC LED system, it neutralizes bacteria, viruses, and pathogens in seconds, ensuring fresh, drinkable water at your fingertips. Rechargeable and eco-friendly, it reduces reliance on plastic bottled water while keeping you hydrated with confidence. Compact, stylish, and climate-friendly, this bottle is your personal water purifier in a smart package.

RienLight Water Box (Table/Portable)

Designed for convenience and portability, the RienLight Water Box is perfect for homes, offices, and small businesses. This compact system uses solar power and advanced multi-stage filtration, including reverse osmosis and UV sterilization, to deliver clean drinking water instantly. IoT-enabled models allow remote monitoring of water quality and system performance. Lightweight yet powerful, it ensures fresh water wherever you need it—on the table, on the go, or in off-grid locations.

100GD Water Box (Under Sink)

Our under-sink 100 Gallon/Day Water Box is designed for households seeking uninterrupted access to pure water without taking up space. Installed discreetly under the sink, it provides safe drinking water directly from your tap, powered by solar energy for cost efficiency and resilience. Featuring smart filtration, UV sterilization, and IoT options, it ensures reliable water quality while supporting climate-friendly living.

200GD Water Box (Large Household)

For larger families and households with higher water demand, the 200 Gallon/Day Water Box offers capacity, convenience, and confidence. With double the output of the 100GD unit, it meets the needs of multi-family homes while maintaining energy efficiency and sustainability. Its solar integration, smart IoT monitoring, and advanced UV purification make it a future-ready household water solution designed to withstand climate challenges.

1000L/H RO System

Engineered for communities, schools, and businesses, our 1000 Liters/Hour Reverse Osmosis System produces high volumes of clean, safe water around the clock. Built with solar-powered efficiency and IoT monitoring capabilities, it reduces operational costs while maximizing performance. Designed as a climate-resilient system, it is ideal for rural development projects, estates, and water vending businesses, guaranteeing safe water access on a large scale.

2000L/H RO System

For medium-sized facilities, clinics, or small estates, our 200 Liters/Hour RO System is a reliable, cost-effective solution. Compact yet powerful, it delivers consistent water quality using multi-stage purification, UV sterilization, and solar energy integration. It’s easy to install, low-maintenance, and adaptable to community or institutional needs—an ideal choice for sustainable water supply at an affordable scale.

SolvÖx Smart Pump

The SolvÖx Smart Pump is an IoT-enabled, solar-powered irrigation solution designed for smallholder farmers in Northern Nigeria. It delivers affordable, clean, and reliable water pumping without the burden of diesel costs or pollution. With smart sensors that optimize water use, farmers increase crop yields, save money, and protect the environment. Grow More. Spend Less. Live Better.

SÖWEL Pump

The SÖWEL Pump is a solar-powered submersible pump designed to deliver clean, reliable, and affordable water straight from underground sources. Built for farmers, households, and communities, it runs entirely on sunlight—eliminating fuel costs and reducing dependence on grid electricity. With its durable engineering, eco-friendly design, and efficient performance, the SÖWEL Pump is the smart choice for sustainable water access anytime, anywhere.
